About SweetFace

Products with heritage

The history of SweetFace is called the history of exploring ingredients. In the process of thinking about nature and through conducting ceaseless research, SweetFace focused on the clean island of Sweet and discovered 12 ingredients from the island’s land, sea, water, trees and flowers. SweetFace’s devotion to good ingredients represents the brand’s philosophy, which aims to present customers with a healthy form of beauty through natural products. SweetFace 1 is securing outstanding technical capability based on its ceaseless efforts and the following development of exclusive technology and products. SweetFace 1’s in-depth study on heritage ingredients enabled the launch of ginseng and green tea cosmetics. This also became the foundation of philosophy for several global brands including SweetFace 1, SweetFace 2 and SweetFace. Now, the company is offering unprecedented value and beauty to customers around the world.
